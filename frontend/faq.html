FAQs

What is Girivan Hikes? ▼

Girivan Hikes is a platform where trekkers can connect with guides, explore trekking blogs, and find safe trekking opportunities.

How can I contact a trekking guide? ▼

You can search for treks based on location and difficulty level, and use our chat feature to contact guides directly.

Are there safety measures in place? ▼

Yes, we prioritize safety by ensuring all guides are verified and provide emergency contact information for all treks.

Is the platform free to use? ▼

Creating an account and browsing treks is free. Some guides may charge fees for their services.

How do I sign up for Girivan Hikes? ▼

Click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and fill in your details to create a free account.

Can I organize treks as a guide? ▼

Yes, verified guides can create trekking events, specify schedules, and attract participants to join their treks.

What types of treks are available on Girivan Hikes? ▼

We offer treks of various difficulty levels ranging from beginner to expert, including day hikes, mountain expeditions, and cultural walks.

Can I cancel a booking? ▼

Yes, cancellations can be made through your account. Refund policies depend on the guide’s terms and conditions.

How do I write a blog on Girivan Hikes? ▼

Once logged in, go to the "Write a Blog" section, share your trekking experiences, and publish your article for others to read.

Do you have customer support? ▼

Yes, our customer support team is available 24/7. You can reach out through the "Contact Us" page for assistance.
